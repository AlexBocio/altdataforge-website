About

One person. Public data. Refined.

Alt Data Forge is an independent data refinery. It takes public bulk data — patents, regulatory filings, short-volume archives, federal contracts, lobbying records, congressional trades, clinical trials, and more — and refines it into clean ticker-keyed daily panels for buy-side analysts who don't want to run their own ETL. No alpha claims, no dashboards, no signals — just the cleaned raw material your quants build on.

Stock bundles or custom builds. ML-ready, not signal-ready.

US Army/AirForce Veteran. Self-taught builder. Based in New Jersey. No investors, no team, no roadmap deck — just a working pipeline and an inbox.
