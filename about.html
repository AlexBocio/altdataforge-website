About Alt Data Forge

Alt Data Forge is an independent data refinery focused on transforming fragmented public records into point-in-time research datasets.

We work primarily with public regulatory, financial market-structure, and healthcare data sources, including FINRA RegSHO archives, SEC EDGAR, USPTO records, and related public datasets.

Our focus is not prediction marketing, retail signals, or packaged “alpha.”

Our focus is:

  • temporal integrity
  • provenance
  • reproducibility
  • identifier resolution
  • schema consistency
  • institutional usability

Every dataset is designed around the assumption that serious research teams will test, challenge, and integrate the data into their own models and workflows.

We believe the edge is not raw data itself.
The edge is disciplined synthesis.

Philosophy

Most public data is fragmented, revised, poorly timestamped, and difficult to operationalize at scale.

Alt Data Forge exists to clean that problem up.

We build research-ready datasets with:

  • point-in-time consistency
  • bitemporal lineage
  • transparent provenance
  • contract-ready delivery formats

Independent by design.
Built for research teams that value evidence over narratives.
